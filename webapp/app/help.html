Job notation

Wrapped job group
Asterisk, annotation
Light gray, pending
Gray, running
Green, success
Orange, warnings
Purple, infrastructure failure
Red, failure
Blue, build restarted
Pink, build cancelled
Black, unknown error

Keyboard shortcuts

esc Close all open job or filter panels
spacebar Add selected job to the pin board
j or n Highlight next unstarred failure
k or p Highlight previous unstarred failure
u Show only unstarred failures
i Toggle in-progress (running/pending) jobs
ctrl/cmd click Add job to the pinboard
